Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a premier private deemed university in India with campuses in Vellore, Chennai, Andhra Pradesh, and Bhopal, consistently ranked among the top engineering institutions (11th in NIRF 2023). Founded in 1984, it features a fully flexible credit system, strong international partnerships, and high placement records, serving over 87,000 students.
